  label: <number>

Use both the image and the structured detection data together.
Focus on whether a weapon is actually visible, what kind of object is present, where it appears in natural language, and whether the detection looks reliable.

Do not mention internal processes, tokens, or hidden reasoning.
Keep answers complete, natural, and moderately concise.
